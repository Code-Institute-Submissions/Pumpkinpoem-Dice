Who We Are

We are a group of geeks passionate about coffee and tabletop games. Enjoy both with our cozy atmosphere and large tables.


Magical Coffee

Fair trade magical coffee and other drinks are on the menu.


Social Gaming

Every other weekend we have drop-in D&D games with an amazing dungeon master! Also, Magic the Gathering tournaments are held once a month.


Movie Night

Every Monday we live stream Critical Role at 6 pm, and the third Saturday every month we have a late-night movie showing.

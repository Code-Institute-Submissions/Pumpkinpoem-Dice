The One Dice

Gaming Cafe in Rijeka, Croatia

Welcome

Who We Are

We are a group of geeks passionate about coffee and tabletop games. With our cozy atmosphere and large tables, there is ample room for even the largest tabletop games out there.

Magical Coffee

Fair trade magical coffee and other drinks are on the menu.

Social Gaming

Every other weekend we have drop-in D&D games with an amazing dungeon master, and Magic: The Gathering tournaments once a month.

Movie Night

Every Monday we livestream Critical Role at 6 PM, and the third Saturday every month we have a late-night movie showing.
